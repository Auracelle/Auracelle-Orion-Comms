Platform Overview

Two domains.
One governance picture.

Most cyber wargaming tools collapse physical infrastructure and software supply chains into a single risk score — producing results too generic for institutional use. Auracelle Orion separates them, scores them independently, and models precisely how failure travels between them.

IFD · Infrastructure Domain
Physical & OT Infrastructure

Physical plant, OT/ICS/SCADA systems, network hardware, power and cooling systems. Scored across Physical Integrity, Operational Resilience, Network Segmentation, and Hardware Provenance.

SWD · Software Domain
Software & Cloud Systems

Application stacks, firmware, cloud-native services, CI/CD pipelines, and dependency graphs. Scored across SBOM Visibility, Dependency Trust, Patch Velocity, and CI/CD Integrity.

SCTL · Supply-Chain Trust Layer
Supply-Chain Propagation

The directed trust graph connecting vendors, integrators, and operators. Models how compromise propagates across domains and sectors — SBOM disclosure, vendor concentration, patch latency, grey-market risk.

"Without decomposition, after-action reports say the response was poor. With Orion, they say exactly which governance failure drove the cascade — and what a different decision would have cost."
Grace-Alice Evans  ·  Director & Principal Investigator, Auracelle AI Governance Labs
Core Capabilities

From visible to measurable.
From symptoms to causes.

Orion transforms simulation from training exercise into policy investment justification — giving institutions the evidence to act before a crisis, not after.

01
Quantified Governance Scoring

Every scenario produces a traceable governance score across both domains. High scores decompose to their source — whether in SBOM visibility, patch latency, or IT/OT segmentation failure.

02
Counterfactual Policy Analysis

Re-run any scenario with a different configuration. The difference in scores between runs is the measurable governance value of a specific policy investment — expressed in outcome, not opinion.

03
Accelerated Decision-Making

Domain-partitioned time acceleration compresses the full incident lifecycle — from detection through governance consolidation — into a structured environment that quantifies decision quality under pressure.

04
Live Data Integration

Real-time data from World Bank, SIPRI, UN Comtrade, and US Export Controls grounds every scenario in current geopolitical and technical conditions rather than static assumptions.

Who It's For

Built for institutions
that govern at scale.

Orion is designed for decision-makers who need evidence, not dashboards — and for institutions whose governance choices have consequences beyond their own perimeter.

⬡
National Governments & Regulators

Assess cyber governance readiness across critical sectors. Justify policy investments with counterfactual evidence. Map scores directly to regulatory frameworks including NIS2 and US EO 14028.

⬡
International Institutions

A common simulation framework for multilateral crisis exercises. Scenario outputs carry confidence bounds suitable for policy deliberation at NATO, UN, OECD, and IAEA level.

⬡
Critical Infrastructure Operators

Identify which governance investments change outcomes under your specific threat profile. Separate OT and software response playbooks. Quantify supply-chain exposure before an incident forces the question.
